Custom & Repairs

Bring us the gold. Bring us the story.

A clasp that finally gave out. A grandmother's ring that no longer fits. Loose stones you have been afraid to wear. We handle the small jobs most people put off for years, and we design new pieces from scratch when you are ready to make something of your own.

01

Custom design

Tell us the piece in your head. We sketch it, quote it honestly, and build it in gold or platinum, your stones or ours.

02

Repairs & restoration

Ring sizing, chain and clasp repair, prong rebuilding, re-tipping, polishing, and rhodium plating that makes white gold bright again.

03

Stone setting & resetting

Reset an inherited diamond into something you will actually wear, or replace a stone that fell out years ago.

04

Watch batteries & appraisals

Quick battery swaps while you wait, plus straightforward appraisals for insurance and peace of mind.
